<h2>Undergraduate Thesis</h2>

**Fluctuation-Response Behavior of Non-equilibrium Brownian Motor**

Numerical verification of linear response theory and fluctuation theorems in an underdamped Langevin system, and design of a programmable magnetic-field control scheme using an electromagnet array.

**Key Contributions:**

- Numerical consistency check between thermodynamic and trajectory entropy production
- Inertial correction to TUR: underdamped Q ≈ 0.80 vs. overdamped Q ≈ 1.00
- Systematic scan of linear response validity range, separating SNR effects from physical nonlinearity
- Harada–Sasa "trivial NESS" analysis for a free OU process
- Least-squares inverse design of multi-coil electromagnet array (Tikhonov regularization)
- Thermodynamic applications: optimal transport, Landauer erasure, Brownian Carnot engine
