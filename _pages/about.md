## 研究方向 | Research Interests

<div style="display: flex; gap: 2em; flex-wrap: wrap;">
  <div style="flex: 1; min-width: 280px;">
    <h3>随机热力学 | Stochastic Thermodynamics</h3>
    <p>
      在单粒子轨道层面定义功、热与熵产生，研究远离平衡的随机过程中热力学量的概率分布及时间反演对称性。
      主要框架：Sekimoto–Seifert 随机热力学体系。
    </p>
    <p>
      Defining work, heat, and entropy production along individual trajectories; studying probability distributions
      and time-reversal symmetries of thermodynamic quantities far from equilibrium (Sekimoto–Seifert framework).
    </p>
  </div>
  <div style="flex: 1; min-width: 280px;">
    <h3>线性响应理论 | Linear Response Theory</h3>
    <p>
      研究欠阻尼布朗转子在弱扰动下的响应函数，验证涨落–耗散定理（FDT）及其在非平衡稳态中的适用边界；
      分析线性响应的有效范围。
    </p>
    <p>
      Investigating response functions of an underdamped Brownian rotor under weak perturbations;
      verifying the fluctuation–dissipation theorem (FDT) and its validity boundary in nonequilibrium
      steady states; characterizing the range of linear response.
    </p>
  </div>
  <div style="flex: 1; min-width: 280px;">
    <h3>涨落定理 & TUR | Fluctuation Theorems & TUR</h3>
    <p>
      数值验证热力学熵产生与轨迹熵产生的一致性；检验热力学不确定性关系（TUR）并量化欠阻尼惯性效应对
      精度–能耗权衡的修正（欠阻尼 Q ≈ 0.80，过阻尼 Q ≈ 1.00）。
    </p>
    <p>
      Numerically verifying consistency between thermodynamic and trajectory entropy production;
      testing TUR and quantifying the inertial correction to precision–dissipation tradeoff
      (underdamped Q ≈ 0.80 vs. overdamped Q ≈ 1.00).
    </p>
  </div>
  <div style="flex: 1; min-width: 280px;">
    <h3>磁场主动调控 | Magnetic Field Control</h3>
    <p>
      设计基于电磁铁阵列的可编程磁场系统，通过最小二乘逆向拟合在工作区域内构造阶跃势场与棘轮势，
      为有限时间最优输运、信息擦除与布朗卡诺热机等应用奠定基础。
    </p>
    <p>
      Designing a programmable magnetic-field control system using an electromagnet array; constructing
      step-function potentials and ratchet potentials via least-squares inverse fitting; enabling
      applications to optimal transport, Landauer information erasure, and Brownian Carnot engines.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>
