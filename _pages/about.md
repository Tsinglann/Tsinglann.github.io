<h1>关于我</h1>

<p>
我是兰州大学物理科学与技术学院理论物理专业 2022 级本科生，指导教师为董家奇副教授。
我的研究兴趣集中于<strong>非平衡统计物理</strong>与<strong>随机热力学</strong>，
具体包括欠阻尼朗之万系统的线性响应、涨落定理、热力学不确定性关系（TUR）以及外场主动调控。
</p>

<p>毕业论文题目：<strong>《非平衡布朗转子的涨落响应行为研究》</strong></p>

<hr>

<h2>研究方向</h2>

<div style="display:flex;gap:1.5em;flex-wrap:wrap;">
  <div style="flex:1;min-width:260px;border-left:3px solid #4a90d9;padding-left:1em;">
    <h3>随机热力学</h3>
    <p>在单粒子轨道层面定义功、热与熵产生，研究远离平衡的随机过程中热力学量的概率分布及时间反演对称性（Sekimoto–Seifert 框架）。</p>
  </div>
  <div style="flex:1;min-width:260px;border-left:3px solid #4a90d9;padding-left:1em;">
    <h3>线性响应理论</h3>
    <p>研究欠阻尼布朗转子在弱扰动下的响应函数，验证涨落–耗散定理（FDT）及其在非平衡稳态中的适用边界，分析线性响应的有效范围。</p>
  </div>
  <div style="flex:1;min-width:260px;border-left:3px solid #4a90d9;padding-left:1em;">
    <h3>涨落定理 &amp; TUR</h3>
    <p>数值验证热力学熵产生与轨迹熵产生的一致性；检验热力学不确定性关系并量化欠阻尼惯性效应（欠阻尼 Q ≈ 0.80，过阻尼 Q ≈ 1.00）。</p>
  </div>
  <div style="flex:1;min-width:260px;border-left:3px solid #4a90d9;padding-left:1em;">
    <h3>磁场主动调控</h3>
    <p>设计基于电磁铁阵列的可编程磁场系统，通过最小二乘逆向拟合构造阶跃势场与棘轮势，为最优输运、信息擦除与布朗卡诺热机提供平台。</p>
  </div>
</div>

<h1>About Me</h1>

<p>
I am a senior undergraduate student (Class of 2022) majoring in Theoretical Physics at the School of Physical Science and Technology, Lanzhou University, advised by Associate Prof. Jiaqi Dong. My research focuses on <strong>nonequilibrium statistical physics</strong> and <strong>stochastic thermodynamics</strong>, including linear response theory, fluctuation theorems, thermodynamic uncertainty relations (TUR), and active magnetic-field control of potential landscapes.
</p>

<p>Thesis: <strong><em>Fluctuation-Response Behavior of Non-equilibrium Brownian Motor</em></strong></p>

<hr>

<h2>Research Interests</h2>

<div style="display:flex;gap:1.5em;flex-wrap:wrap;">
  <div style="flex:1;min-width:260px;border-left:3px solid #4a90d9;padding-left:1em;">
    <h3>Stochastic Thermodynamics</h3>
    <p>Defining work, heat, and entropy production along individual trajectories; studying probability distributions and time-reversal symmetries of thermodynamic quantities far from equilibrium (Sekimoto–Seifert framework).</p>
  </div>
  <div style="flex:1;min-width:260px;border-left:3px solid #4a90d9;padding-left:1em;">
    <h3>Linear Response Theory</h3>
    <p>Investigating response functions of an underdamped Brownian rotor under weak perturbations; verifying the fluctuation–dissipation theorem (FDT) and its validity boundary in nonequilibrium steady states.</p>
  </div>
  <div style="flex:1;min-width:260px;border-left:3px solid #4a90d9;padding-left:1em;">
    <h3>Fluctuation Theorems &amp; TUR</h3>
    <p>Numerically verifying consistency of thermodynamic and trajectory entropy production; testing TUR and quantifying the inertial correction (underdamped Q ≈ 0.80 vs. overdamped Q ≈ 1.00).</p>
  </div>
  <div style="flex:1;min-width:260px;border-left:3px solid #4a90d9;padding-left:1em;">
    <h3>Magnetic Field Control</h3>
    <p>Designing a programmable magnetic-field system using an electromagnet array; constructing step-function and ratchet potentials via least-squares inverse fitting for optimal transport, information erasure, and Carnot engines.</p>
  </div>
</div>
